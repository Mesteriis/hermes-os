# Code of Conduct

Contributors are expected to keep discussion technical, respectful and focused
on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and constructive.
- Assume privacy and safety constraints matter.
- Critique code, design and tradeoffs rather than people.
- Keep reports and examples free of private user data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private communications, documents, credentials or personal data.
- Deliberately bypassing project security or safety boundaries.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, close discussions or block contributors whose
behavior harms the project or its contributors.
